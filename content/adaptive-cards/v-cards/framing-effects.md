## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Category
Prompt Psychology

### Purpose
Understand and strategically use phrasing, order, and emotional tone to influence how the AI interprets and responds.

### Core Concepts
   - The same task can yield different results depending on phrasing.
   - Can emphasize optimism, caution, curiosity, etc.
   - Useful for leadership tone, UX messaging, or narrative design.

### Prompting Applications
   - “What’s a positive way to explain this?” vs. “What might go wrong?”
   - Vary opening tone or perspective to affect the AI’s disposition.

### Prompt Fragment Example
    Imagine you’re presenting this to a hopeful audience. How would you reframe the message?
