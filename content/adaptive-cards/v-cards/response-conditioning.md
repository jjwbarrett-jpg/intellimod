## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Category
Output Shaping

### Purpose
Steer the AI's response format, style, or tone using targeted signal phrases or priming techniques.

### Core Concepts
   - Influences how the AI responds without altering core instructions.
   - Often includes examples or sentence starters.
   - Can be placed before or after the main instruction.

### Prompting Applications
   - “Respond in 3 bullet points or fewer.”
   - “Start your answer with ‘According to recent studies…’”
   - Aligns output with user expectations or platform norms.

### Prompt Fragment Example
    Begin your reply with a metaphor and conclude with a reflective summary.
