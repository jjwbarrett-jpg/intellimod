## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Category
Intent Coordination

### Purpose
Handle prompts with multiple user goals or layers of intent by segmenting and sequencing them logically.

### Core Concepts
   - Prevents conflict or confusion when multiple actions are requested.
   - Clarifies user expectations and reduces misinterpretation.
   - Helps AI prioritize and sequence tasks efficiently.

### Prompting Applications
   - First summarize the document, then translate it to Spanish.
   - Analyze the tone, identify any logical fallacies, and suggest improvements.
   - Mark or segment each sub-task for clarity.
