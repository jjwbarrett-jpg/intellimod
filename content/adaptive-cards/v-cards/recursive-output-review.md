## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Category
Self-Improvement Looping

### Purpose
Prompt the AI to review, critique, and revise its own output based on defined criteria.

### Core Concepts
   - Helps reduce errors or hallucinations.
   - Encourages multi-pass refinement for quality control.
   - Mimics expert review or editor workflows.

### Prompting Applications
   - “After writing, evaluate your response against the goals.”
   - “Revise anything unclear, excessive, or off-topic.”

### Prompt Fragment Example
    Now reread what you wrote. What would a second-pass reviewer change or improve?
