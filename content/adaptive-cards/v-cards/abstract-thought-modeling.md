## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Category
High-Level Cognitive Emulation

### Purpose
Simulate abstract, philosophical, or conceptual reasoning typically associated with human higher-order thinking.

### Core Concepts
   - Goes beyond factual or procedural tasks into inference and metaphor.
   - Encourages synthesis, analogies, or thought experiments.
   - Useful for advanced creative ideation or speculative exploration.

### Prompting Applications
   - Ask the AI to interpret meaning, predict consequences, or pose hypothetical scenarios.
   - Drive deeper exploration of subjective or vague concepts.

### Prompt Fragment Example
"What might this concept look like if visualized as a landscape, system, or metaphor?"
