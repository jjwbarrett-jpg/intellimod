## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Category
Intent Alignment

### Core Function
Forces the model to first rephrase a user's prompt in its own words to clarify intent, then answer it.

### Definition
Rephrase-and-Respond prompts guide the AI to paraphrase or expand the user's input before generating a reply. This pre-processing step improves comprehension, reduces ambiguity, and increases the relevance of the output.

### Example

**Prompt:** Rephrase the following question to clarify the user's intent. Then respond.
User Input: Why did Rome fall?
Rephrased: You are asking for the primary reasons the Roman Empire collapsed. Here is a concise summary...

### Trigger Phrases
   - Reword the prompt in clearer terms.
   - Clarify what the user is really asking.
   - Restate then respond.
   - Expand the question before answering.

### Related Concepts
   - Self-Reflection
   - Meta Prompting
   - Active Clarification
   - EmotionPrompting

### Use Cases
   - Novice users with vague prompts
   - Multi-language or ESL prompting
   - Customer support chatbots
   - Clarifying emotional, complex, or goal-driven queries
