## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Short Definition
Text-to-Image Prompting involves crafting structured, balanced, and descriptive language that guides AI models (e.g., Midjourney, DALL-E, Firefly) to produce visual outputs aligned with creative or technical goals.

### Domain Focus
   - **Primary:** Multimodal Prompting
   - **Subdomains:** visual language design, prompt anatomy, image rendering logic

### Utility for AI
   - Directs visual composition, subject focus, and style
   - Boosts realism, consistency, or stylization
   - Enables character, scene, and concept generation

### Related Methods
   - Seed Locking
   - Negative Prompting
   - Visual Prompt Modifiers

### Use Cases
   - Concept art and character sheets
   - Product mockups
   - Visual storytelling
