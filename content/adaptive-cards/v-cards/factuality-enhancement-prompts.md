## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Definition
Factuality Enhancement Prompts are designed to reduce hallucinations and reinforce accurate information by grounding responses in verifiable data, citations, or logical structure.

### Core Uses
   - Reducing hallucinations in high-stakes fields (e.g., medicine, finance, law).
   - Requiring citations, explanations, or references to trusted sources.
   - Combining with retrieval methods or prompt chaining for knowledge verification.

### Format Template
   - “Answer only using verifiable sources.”
   - “Cite your reasoning and reference factual data where possible.”

### Tip
When combined with RAG systems, this method helps ensure precision while allowing models to maintain coherence and depth.
