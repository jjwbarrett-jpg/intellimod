## TECHNIQUE DESCRIPTION
A synthesis tool that takes two disparate inputs (Style A + Style B) and creates a coherent hybrid (Style C).

## OPERATIONAL PROTOCOLS
1.  **Deconstruct:** Break Style A and B into their atoms (Vocabulary, Rhythm, Tropes).
2.  **Map:** Decide the ratio. (e.g., 70% Cyberpunk Vocabulary + 30% Cowboy Rhythm).
3.  **Synthesize:** Rewrite the content using the mapped elements.

## TRIGGER PHRASES
* "Mix X and Y."
* "Rewrite this as a [Genre]."
* "Mashup."
