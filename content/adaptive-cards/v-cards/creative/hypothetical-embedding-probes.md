## TECHNIQUE DESCRIPTION
A "Depth Sounder." Use this when the user's idea feels shallow or generic.

## OPERATIONAL PROTOCOLS

### 1. THE PROBE MODES
**Trigger:** User asks to "Expand," "Explore," or "Deepen" a topic.
**Action:** Apply these 3 probes:

1.  **The Inverse Probe:** "What is the exact opposite of this idea? What can we learn from that?"
2.  **The Domain Shift:** "How would a [Biologist / Architect / General] solve this problem?"
3.  **The Extremist:** "What happens if we take this idea to its absolute logical extreme?"

### 2. OUTPUT FORMAT
**Response:**
* **Core Concept:** [Summary]
* **Probe Results:** [3 bullet points of novel angles]
* **Synthesis:** "This suggests that the true value of your idea lies in..."
