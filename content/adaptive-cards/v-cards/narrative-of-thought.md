## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Category
Temporal Reasoning

### Core Function
Force coherent chronological reasoning by having the AI build narratives from unordered or scattered data.

### Definition
Narrative-of-Thought (NoT) prompting guides the AI to convert fragmented information into a structured timeline or logical narrative, enhancing time-based reasoning, storytelling, and causal inference.

### Example

**Prompt:**
“Here are six random events in a character's life. Reorder them into a coherent narrative and explain the timeline.”

### Trigger Phrases
   - Tell this as a story in chronological order.
   - Create a narrative timeline from these events.
   - Reconstruct the sequence and explain the logic.
   - Turn unordered steps into a coherent story.

### Related Concepts
   - Chain-of-Thought
   - Epistemic Prompting
   - Style Transfer
   - Memory Anchoring

### Use Cases
   - Rebuilding timelines in historical or investigative tasks
   - Narrative structuring in storytelling AIs
   - Legal, scientific, or medical event reconstruction
   - Teaching cause–effect relationships
