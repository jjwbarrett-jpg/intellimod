## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Short Definition
Role Design is the act of assigning a specific identity, persona, or task-oriented role to an AI. This constrains tone, behavior, and reasoning style to suit the intended outcome (e.g., “You are a safety advisor,” “Act as a character expert”).

### Domain Focus
   - **Primary:** Prompt Engineering Techniques
   - **Subdomains:** identity framing, behavioral alignment, output shaping

### Utility for AI
   - Improves output consistency and tone
   - Anchors responses to task-specific expertise
   - Boosts performance in context-limited prompts

### Related Methods
   - Persona Prompting
   - System Prompts
   - Style Matching

### Use Cases
   - Specialized advisors (e.g., lawyer, doctor, historian)
   - Narrative agents or characters
   - Tiered AI systems with defined functions

### Prompt Fragment
    You are a [domain] expert helping a user with [task].
