## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Short Definition
Visual Prompting is the practice of using descriptive language, image references, or visual tags to guide image-generation or perception-based AI models. It shapes how the AI interprets and composes visuals, controlling aspects like style, composition, color, and subject behavior.

### Domain Focus
   - **Primary fields:** Multimodal Prompting (Image/Video/Voice)
   - **Subdomains (optional):** Image synthesis, stylistic control, visual-language bridging

### Use Case Triggers
**Triggers:** "generate an image of", "describe the scene", "in the style of"

### Related scenarios:
   - Creating digital art, concept design, or product mockups
   - Translating mood boards or reference images into AI outputs
   - Combining text + image understanding in hybrid tasks

Key Associations
   - **Keywords:** image prompt, art description, composition guidance, stylization
   - **Related concepts:** Negative Prompting, Seed Locking, Stable Diffusion, Midjourney, DALL-E, Adobe Firefly

### Typical AI Biases
   - Literal misinterpretation of abstract terms
   - Overuse of popular visual motifs
   - Inconsistency between images if not anchored

### Optional Metadata
   - **Recommended P-Cards:** Image Prompt Formatter, Style Anchor, Composition Balancer
   - **Tier Activation:** 1 (silent), 2 (suggested when using image generators), 3 (optional display in visual workflow UI)
   - **Notes:** Works best when paired with consistent stylistic language or visual seeds across prompt iterations.
