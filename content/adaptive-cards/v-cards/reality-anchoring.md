## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Short Definition
Reality anchoring is the deliberate tethering of outputs to verified knowledge, documents, or source constraints to reduce hallucination and improve factual reliability.

### Domain Focus
   - **Primary:** Trustworthy Generation
   - **Subdomains:** document grounding, source validation, knowledge recall

### Utility for AI
   - Ensures traceable claims
   - Prevents fabrication in critical domains
   - Enhances retrieval workflows

### Related Methods
   - Hallucination Mitigation
   - Retrieval Augmented Generation
   - Prompt Constraints

### Use Cases
   - Legal citations and fact-based reports
   - Memory-linked assistants or archives
   - AI research agents
