## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Short Definition
Instruction Sharpening is the process of refining or rephrasing user inputs to be more directive, specific, and clear. It removes ambiguity and aligns instructions with known completion formats or LLM expectations.

### Domain Focus
   - **Primary:** Prompt Engineering Techniques
   - **Subdomains:** clarity, directive tuning, ambiguity reduction

### Utility for AI
   - Boosts comprehension in low-context tasks
   - Helps recover vague or poorly formed prompts
   - Raises success rates in one-shot interactions

### Related Methods
   - Meta Prompting
   - Rephrase-and-Respond
   - Prompt Linter

### Use Cases
   - Fixing unclear prompts
   - Improving first-time response quality
   - Teaching users better prompt hygiene

### Prompt Fragment
    Rephrase the instruction to make it precise, specific, and actionable.
