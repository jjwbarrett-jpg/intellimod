## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Category
Stylistic Alignment

### Purpose
Match or adapt to the user's tone, syntax, and stylistic preferences in response generation.

### Core Concepts
   - Reinforces continuity, emotional tone, and relational trust.
   - Especially useful for roleplay, dialog agents, or branding.
   - Can be explicit (“Match this tone”) or inferred via training data.

### Prompting Applications
   - “Respond in a style similar to how I wrote this…”
   - “Maintain my informal/technical/professional tone.”

### Prompt Fragment Example
    Mirror the writing style above: concise, confident, and slightly humorous.
