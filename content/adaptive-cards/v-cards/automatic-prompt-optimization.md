## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Short Definition
Automatic Prompt Optimization refers to AI-driven methods for improving prompts on the fly—adjusting language, structure, or tokens for clarity, relevance, or performance, often without user intervention.

### Domain Focus
   - **Primary:** Prompt Engineering Techniques
   - **Subdomains:** Meta prompting, response tuning, preprocessor logic

### Utility for AI

   - Enables adaptive, context-aware completions
   - Ideal for dynamic environments or user-facing apps
