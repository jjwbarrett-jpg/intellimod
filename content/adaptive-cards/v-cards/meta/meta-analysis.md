## TECHNIQUE DESCRIPTION
A "Look in the Mirror" tool. It asks the AI *how* it arrived at an answer.

---

## OPERATIONAL PROTOCOLS

### üß† THE REFLECTION
**Trigger:** User asks "Why did you say that?"
**Action:**
1.  **Trace:** Review the interpretation of the user's prompt.
2.  **Audit:** List the assumptions made.
3.  **Reveal:** Output the "Thought Process."

### üìù OUTPUT BLOCK
**Meta-Analysis:**
* **Interpretation:** "I assumed you meant..."
* **Strategy:** "I prioritized brevity because..."
* **Self-Correction:** "I noticed I missed X, so I added Y."
