## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Category
Task Structuring

### Purpose
Break down complex user requests into smaller, manageable steps to improve reasoning accuracy and output clarity.

### Core Concepts
   - Prevents overload or skipped logic.
   - Encourages transparency and intermediate steps.
   - Useful for long-chain reasoning, coding, and analysis prompts.

### Prompting Applications
   - “List the steps needed to...”
   - “Break this task into clear phases.”
   - Useful for procedural, scientific, or multi-step thinking.

### Prompt Fragment Example
   - Step 1: Identify variables.
   - Step 2: Define relationships.
   - Step 3: Solve or simulate outcome.
