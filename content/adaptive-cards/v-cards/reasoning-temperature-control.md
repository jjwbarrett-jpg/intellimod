### AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Category
Precision vs. Creativity Modulation

### Purpose
Adjust how exploratory or conservative the AI’s reasoning is, depending on task type or user needs.

### Core Concepts
   - **High temperature:** creative, abstract, speculative.
   - **Low temperature:** cautious, grounded, analytical.
   - Functions like a tone/logic dial in prompt design.

### Prompting Applications
   - Be bold and creative in proposing unusual solutions.
   - Keep your reasoning strict and fact-based.

### Prompt Fragment Example
    Approach this problem with a high-risk/high-reward mindset. Creativity encouraged — precision optional.
