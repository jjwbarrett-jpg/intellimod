## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Short Definition
Self-Reflection is the act of prompting the AI to assess, critique, or re-evaluate its own reasoning, assumptions, or outputs to improve quality or correct bias.

### Domain Focus
   - **Primary:** Meta-Cognition and Prompt Evaluation
   - **Subdomains:** output critique, bias detection, self-improvement

### Utility for AI
   - Improves coherence, ethics, and structure
   - Supports AI self-debugging
   - Encourages more thoughtful replies

### Related Methods
   - Recursive Prompting
   - Epistemic Prompting
   - ICE Framework

### Use Cases
   - Deep philosophical or ethical analysis
   - Complex planning and scenario testing
   - Tutor-style guidance from AI

### Prompt Fragment
    Evaluate your answer for logic, clarity, and possible bias. Then revise it if needed.
