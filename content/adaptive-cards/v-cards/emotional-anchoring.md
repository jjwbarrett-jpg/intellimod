## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Category
Tone & Bias Priming

### Core Function
Uses emotionally charged context to stabilize reasoning tone, influence decision framing, or enhance engagement.

### Definition
By embedding emotional cues (e.g., “this deeply affects me”) or specific moods (e.g., “you are calm and empathetic”), the model is anchored to a stable affective orientation, influencing everything from tone to ethical reasoning.

### Example

**Prompt:**
“This topic is very important to my family. Please explain with care and empathy.”

### Trigger Phrases
   - “This issue affects real people—respond kindly.”
   - “Use a calm, grounded tone. This matters.”
   - “You’re helping someone in crisis. Be clear and compassionate.”
   - “Approach this as if it were personal.”
