## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Category
EmotionPrompting

### Definition
EmotionPrompting is the strategic use of emotional tone, sentiment, or affective framing to steer the AI's response style—whether to evoke empathy, urgency, caution, or other human-aligned emotional responses.

### Domain Focus
   - **Primary:** Prompt Engineering Techniques
   - **Subdomains:** sentiment alignment, tone guidance, affective priming

### Utility for AI
   - Helps modulate tone across professional, friendly, urgent, or calming contexts
   - Makes outputs feel more human-aligned
   - Useful for storytelling, support systems, and roleplay

### Related Methods
   - Role Design
   - Style Matching
   - Persona Prompting

### Use Cases
   - Therapy simulations
   - Support chatbots
   - Character development with emotional realism

### Prompt Fragment
    Respond with gentle empathy as if consoling someone in distress.
