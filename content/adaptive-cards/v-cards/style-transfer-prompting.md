## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Short Definition
Style Transfer Prompting enables the transformation of an AI's output from one tone, format, or genre into another, while preserving meaning and intention.

### Domain Focus
   - **Primary:** Output Transformation
   - **Subdomains:** voice morphing, tone control, narrative flexibility

### Utility for AI
   - Supports brand voice alignment
   - Adjusts tone for different audiences
   - Bridges creative and technical domains

### Related Methods
   - Rewriting
   - Persona Prompting
   - Compression & Expansion

### Use Cases
   - Turn legal text into poetic language
   - Rewrite casual text into formal instructions
   - Localize tone for different cultures or clients

### Prompt Fragment
    Rewrite this response using the tone and language of a noir detective novel.
