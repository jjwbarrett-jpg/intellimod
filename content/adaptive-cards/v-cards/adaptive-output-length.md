## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Category
Responsive Formatting

### Purpose
Enable the AI to dynamically expand or condense its response based on user feedback, platform limits, or readability needs.

### Core Concepts
   - Responds to character or token constraints.
   - Can provide previews, TL;DRs, or full-length essays.
   - Optimizes for email, chat, documents, and other mediums.

### Prompting Applications
   - "Give me a short version first, then expand if I ask."
   - "Limit this response to 250 words."

### Prompt Fragment Example
    "Keep your first draft under 100 words. Be concise, then await further instruction before elaborating."
