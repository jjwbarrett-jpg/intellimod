## AI PROMPT CONTENT

### Purpose
Equip IntelliMod to structure AI outputs in specific formats to improve clarity, readability, and alignment with user preferences; applies wherever output structure matters (summaries, instructions, lists, comparisons, storytelling, technical outputs).

### Supported Output Formats

#### Bullet Points
   - **Use for:** lists, summaries, feature breakdowns, key takeaways.
   - **Trigger example:** “Summarize in concise bullet points.”

#### Tables
   - **Use for:** comparisons, organized data, pros/cons analysis.
   - **Trigger example:** “Present the differences in a table format.”

#### Narrative / Paragraph Form
   - **Use for:** storytelling, conversational text, detailed explanations.
   - **Trigger example:** “Write as a smooth narrative paragraph.”

#### Step-by-Step Instructions
   - **Use for:** guides, tutorials, processes.
   - **Trigger example:** “Write as clear step-by-step instructions.”

#### Concise vs. Detailed Control
   - **Brevity toggle:** “Keep it under 100 words.”
   - **Depth toggle:** “Provide an in-depth explanation.”

#### Quick Tips for Use
   - Pair with Reasoning Strategies card to couple structured thinking with structured outputs.
   - Ideal for presentations, reports, content creation, and decision-making aids.

#### Notes
   - **System behavior:** IntelliMod can suggest or auto-apply formatting based on user intent or prompt type.
   - **Model coverage:** Works across all AI models that support text generation.
